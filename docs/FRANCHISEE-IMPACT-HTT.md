# Franchisee Impact Brief — HTT Brands (Hub Tenant)

**To:** HTT Brands Corporate Team  
**From:** IT Director, Tyler Granlund  
**Date:** May 2026  
**Subject:** Upcoming Security Changes Affecting Cross-Tenant Access

---

## What's Changing

HTT Brands is strengthening identity security across all five tenants in our portfolio. These changes protect your accounts, data, and franchise operations from increasingly sophisticated cyber threats.

### 1. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) — Already Active ✅
HTT already requires MFA for all users. **No action needed from you.**

### 2. Guest Account Review (Coming Q3 2026)
We will begin quarterly reviews of all guest accounts in HTT. Guest accounts that haven't been used in 90+ days will be disabled. If you have external partners who need ongoing access, please let us know.

### 3. Cross-Tenant Access Controls (Coming Q3 2026)
External organizations will no longer be able to collaborate into HTT by default. Access will be allowed only for known, approved partners (Bishops, Frenchies, The Lash Lounge, Delta Crown).

| Action | When | How |

## What Happens If You Don't Act

- Guest accounts inactive for 90+ days will be **disabled** (not deleted — can be reactivated)
- External partners not on the approved list will **lose access** when deny-by-default is deployed
