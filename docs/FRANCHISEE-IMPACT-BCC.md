## What's Changing

HTT Brands is strengthening identity security across all tenants. Here's what's changing for Bishops.

### 1. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) — Coming Soon
MFA will be required for **all BCC users** (currently admin-only). Your MSP (Sui Generis) will deploy this using a security group approach — same method used successfully at The Lash Lounge.

**Timeline:** Post-convention (target July 2026)

### 2. Guest Account Cleanup
BCC currently has only 2 guest accounts — minimal impact expected.

### 3. Legacy Email Protocols Blocked
POP3, SMTP, and IMAP access for external/guest users will be blocked. Modern email clients (Outlook, mobile apps) are **not affected**.

**Timeline:** Q3 2026

---

## What You Need To Do

| Action | When | How |
|--------|------|-----|

## What Happens If You Don't Act

- Users without MFA enrolled **will not be able to sign in** once the policy is enforced
- POP3/IMAP connections **will stop working** for external accounts — switch to Outlook
